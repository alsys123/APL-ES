Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì Will I lose my progress if I close the browser?

No! Your progress is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. You can safely close the browser and return later. However, if you clear your browser data or switch devices, you'll need to import your progress from an exported ZIP file.

‚ùì Can I use multiple devices?

Yes, using the Export/Import feature. Export your progress from one device and import it on another. Note that progress is not automatically synced between devices.

‚ùì What is the passing score?

Each section and the overall exam require 80% or higher to pass. You can view detailed scores in the Grade Exam results.

‚ùì How many questions are in the exam?

The complete exam contains 300 questions divided into 6 sections of 50 questions each. Each section has 10 parts with 5 questions per part.

‚ùì Can I change my answer?

Yes! Click any answer option to select it. Click the same option again to deselect, or click a different option to change your answer. You can modify answers at any time before final submission.

‚ùì What does Auto-Check do?

Auto-Check provides immediate visual feedback. Question cards turn green for correct answers and red for incorrect answers. Red cards also display explanations to help you learn from mistakes.

‚ùì Should I use Auto-Check while studying?

It depends on your learning style:

  • Study mode: Enable Auto-Check to learn from explanations immediately
  • Practice exam mode: Disable Auto-Check to simulate real testing conditions

‚ùì What happens if I forget my password?

Contact your instructor or system administrator to reset your password. Passwords cannot be recovered through the system itself.

‚ùì Can I skip questions and come back later?

Yes! You can navigate freely between all sections and parts. Unanswered questions are tracked, and you can return to them anytime using the navigation tools.

‚ùì Is there a time limit?

No, there is no time limit. Take as long as you need to complete the exam. Your progress is saved continuously.

‚ùì What browsers are supported?

The system works best on modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge (latest versions). Ensure JavaScript and cookies are enabled.

‚ùì Will my exported file work on a different computer?

Yes! The exported ZIP file contains your complete progress and can be imported on any computer running the exam system, as long as you log in with the same username.

‚ùì What does "‚è≥ In Progress" mean?

This indicates you haven't answered all questions in that section or part yet. Complete all questions to see a final pass/fail status.

‚ùì Can I retake the exam?

Yes! You can review and change any answers at any time. Use the navigation tools to revisit specific sections or parts.
